Workflow Tools

Five tools, one portal. Here's exactly when to use each one and what to have ready when you open it.

Tools you trigger
01
Instant Lead Response
New lead comes in from Zillow, Realtor.com, your website, or anywhere else
Open tool →
What to have ready
  • Lead's name, phone, email
  • Their exact message — paste it in, don't paraphrase
  • Property address they asked about
  • Price, beds, baths optional but improves the reply
  • Any strong selling points optional
What happens
Result Claude writes a personalized reply referencing their message and the property — sends it to the lead within 60 seconds. A preview copy lands in your inbox.
One-time setup: Your name, email, and phone save automatically after your first submission. You won't have to re-enter them.
02
Listing Description Generator
New listing — need a headline, MLS description, and social post
Open tool →
What to have ready
  • Property address, price, beds/baths
  • Key features & upgrades — be specific (new roof 2022, quartz counters, in-floor heat)
  • Sq ft, lot size, year built, garage, basement optional but useful
  • Neighborhood notes optional
  • 3–5 of your previous listing descriptions — for voice matching (first time only)
What happens
Result Three outputs land in your inbox within 1–2 minutes: a headline, a full MLS description, and a social media post — all written in your voice.
Voice matching: Paste 3–5 of your previous listings the first time you use it. The tool saves them automatically — you won't be asked again. The more examples you give, the more the output sounds like you.
03
Open House Sign-In
Before an open house — generates a QR code for the door
Open tool →
What to have ready
  • Property address
  • Your MLS listing description — copy/paste the full thing
  • Your name, email, phone
What happens
Result A QR code is emailed to you. Print it and tape it to the door. Attendees scan it and fill in their info. The next morning, each person gets a personalized follow-up automatically.
Day-of workflow: Run this the day before or morning of the open house. Print the QR code — that's all you need to bring. The follow-ups go out the next day without any action from you.
04
Cold Nurture Enrollment
A lead went quiet after initial contact — keep the relationship warm automatically
Open tool →
What to have ready
  • Lead's name, phone, email
  • Their original message or a short summary of your conversation
  • Property they were interested in
  • Price, beds, baths optional
What happens
Result — 6 touches over 21 days
Day 1EmailFollowing up — any questions?
Day 3SMSDifferent angle on the property
Day 7EmailMarket context for this price range
Day 10SMSSoft check-in
Day 14EmailNo pressure — here when ready
Day 21SMSFinal low-pressure touch
Every message is written by Claude using this lead's specific details — not a generic template. You receive copies of everything that goes out.
05
Review Request
After closing — send a warm, personalized ask for a Google (or Zillow) review
Open tool →
What to have ready
  • Client's first name and phone
  • Client's email optional — adds an email send
  • Your Google review link — find it in Google Business Profile → "Ask for reviews"
  • Your Zillow profile link optional
  • Property address and buyer/seller
  • When to send: 1, 3, or 7 days after closing
What happens
Result A personalized SMS (and email if you provided one) goes out at the scheduled time with a direct link to leave a review. Nothing else to do.
One-time setup: Your name and review links save after your first submission. After that, just enter the client's info for each closing.
Runs automatically — no action needed
Always on
Missed Call → Auto SMS

If someone calls your Norr AI number and you don't pick up, they automatically receive a text. During business hours (8 AM–5 PM) and after hours, they get different messages — both let them know you'll be in touch and give them a way to reach you.

Nothing to configure. If you ever want to update the message text, contact hello@norrai.co.
