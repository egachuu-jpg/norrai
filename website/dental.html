Starter tier · Dental

What goes live in the first 2 weeks

These are the workflows we deploy for every dental client on Starter. All of them run automatically — you review and approve before anything goes live.

01
Appointment reminders (48h + 2h)

Two texts per appointment. First at 48 hours — enough time to fill the slot if they cancel. Second at 2 hours — catches day-of forgetters. One-tap confirm link included.

02
Missed appointment follow-up

3-touch follow-up sequence when a patient no-shows. Day of, day after, day three. Reschedule link on every message. Recovers a meaningful percentage even after they missed.

03
Post-visit review request

Timed text after a completed appointment with a direct link to your Google Business profile. The right moment, automatically. No staff action required.

04
Missed call → auto SMS

Every missed call triggers an instant text back — business hours aware. New patients don't move on to the next dental office. They get a response before their car leaves your parking lot.

05
New patient intake via text

New patient calls or fills out a form → automated text sequence collects basic intake info before they arrive. Saves front desk time and makes the first visit smoother.

06
Monthly summary report

What ran, what it recovered, what it generated in reviews and patient responses. In appointment-value terms — not system execution counts.

Dormant patient reactivation

The average dental practice has hundreds of patients who haven't been in for 12+ months. They're not gone — they just haven't been asked.

The Growth tier adds a reactivation workflow: AI identifies patients past their recall window, sequences a warm outreach over 2–3 touches, and books them back in. No staff time involved.
