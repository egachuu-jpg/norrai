Starter tier · Real estate

What goes live in the first 2 weeks

Every workflow runs automatically. You approve the messages and logic before go-live — then the system handles it from there.

01
Instant lead response

New lead in — from Zillow, your website, Facebook, or your CRM — AI reads the listing details and inquiry, sends a personalized reply within 5 minutes. Under 5, every time.

02
Cold lead 7-touch nurture

Not every lead is ready on day one. A 30-day, 7-touch sequence keeps you top of mind — email and SMS — until they're ready to move. Drops off cleanly when they respond.

03
Missed call → auto SMS

Every missed call gets an instant text: "Hey, it's [Your Name] — sorry I missed you. What's the best way to reach you?" Business hours aware. No after-hours blasts.

04
Listing description generator

Fill out a short form with the property details. AI writes your headline, MLS description, and social media post — in your voice, matching your past listings. Done in under 3 minutes.

05
Open house follow-up

3-touch sequence after every open house. Same-day text, 48-hour follow-up, 7-day check-in. Personalized by which listing they attended. None of this requires you to remember who came.

06
Post-close review request

Timed review request after a closed deal — when the client is happiest and most likely to write a glowing review. Direct link to Google Business. No manual follow-up needed.
