This AI Automation Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into between Norr AI LLC ("Norr AI") and the Client named above. By signing below, both parties agree to the terms set forth in this Agreement.

1. Services

Norr AI will configure, deploy, and maintain the following automation workflows on Client's behalf (the "Services"):

  • Instant Lead Response — AI-personalized SMS or email reply sent to new leads within minutes of inquiry, customized by listing and lead source.
  • 7-Touch Cold Nurture Sequence — Automated follow-up sequence across 30–90 days for leads that don't convert immediately.
  • Missed Call → Auto SMS — Automatic text reply to missed calls during and after business hours.
  • Listing Description Generator — AI-drafted MLS description, headline, and social post from property details submitted by Client.
  • Open House Follow-Up — Automated follow-up messages to open house attendees the morning after each event.

Services are delivered using Norr AI's automation infrastructure (n8n, Claude API, Twilio, SendGrid). All workflows are template-based. Custom development beyond the scope above is not included in the Starter tier and may be quoted separately.

3. Term

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues on a month-to-month basis until terminated by either party in accordance with Section 4.

4. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing 30 days' written notice to the other party via email. Upon termination:

  • Norr AI will deactivate Client's workflows at the end of the notice period.
  • No refund will be issued for any partial month already billed.
  • The setup fee is non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • If Client terminates within the first 30 days, the setup fee remains due in full.

Norr AI may terminate immediately and without notice if Client engages in conduct that violates applicable law, Twilio or SendGrid acceptable use policies, or that poses a reputational risk to Norr AI.

5. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

  • Provide accurate lead data, phone numbers, and property information needed to operate the workflows.
  • Obtain any required consents from leads and contacts to receive automated SMS and email communications under applicable law (including TCPA and CAN-SPAM).
  • Review and approve AI-generated content before using it for client-facing communications where manual review is available.
  • Notify Norr AI promptly of any errors, complaints, or opt-out requests received from contacts.
  • Not share login credentials or access links with unauthorized third parties.

Data submitted by Client (lead information, property details, contact lists) remains Client's property. Norr AI will not use Client's data for any purpose other than delivering the Services.
