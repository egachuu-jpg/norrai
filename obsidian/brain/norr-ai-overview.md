## Core stack

- **n8n Cloud** — workflow automation
- **Claude API** — intelligence layer
- **Twilio** — SMS (one subaccount per client)
- **SendGrid** — email from hello@norrai.co
- **Neon (Postgres)** — client data, leads, workflow event log
- **Cloudflare Pages** — website hosting
- **Cloudflare Access** — auth on /clients/* and /internal/*

## Sales principles

- Lead with ROI in the client's language — never with technology
- Dentists think in appointment values. Realtors think in GCI. Brokers think in retained premium.
- Never lead with n8n, Claude, or "automation"
- Salesforce positioning (insurance): "we complete Salesforce, not compete with it"
